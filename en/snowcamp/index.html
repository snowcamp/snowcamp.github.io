SnowCamp

The conference driving you (wal)nuts

What is SnowCamp?

universities, conferences, unconference

First and foremost, it’s a highly technical conference where IT professionals from around the world come to share their knowledge of today’s and tomorrow’s technologies. But SnowCamp is also a team of volunteer geeks driven by the idea of organizing the one and only conference of its kind in Grenoble! Finally, SnowCamp is a concept of its own: conferences and workshops on Thursday and Friday; and on Saturday, a relaxing day in the snow.

Grenoble and the innovation

Grenoble by night

Since the dawn of electricity, Grenoble has always been a hub of innovation and excellence: home to cutting-edge labs and industries, labeled “French Tech” for over 10 years, and now crowned European Capital of Innovation 2026. Here, technical innovation has taken root in the surrounding mountains — and that’s what sets us apart.

Conferences

A talk

SnowCamp is built around three main events:

  • the Conference and workshops, on Thursday and Friday: 20 minutes to 2 hours presentations. In 2017, the conference was structured around several tracks,
  • the Meet & Greet, on Thursday evening: a gathering that gives the opportunity to meet and share with other attendees
  • the Unconference, on Saturday, offers to speakers and attendees an opportunity to meet each other on the slopes of the Grenoble area.

The first edition back in 2016 gathered ~200 attendees and speakers in Grenoble university building, and grew with your support to be hosted in Grenoble’s World Trade Center to gather ~600 attendees and ~70 speakers since 2020. Since 2026, SnowCamp has been held at Espace 1968 in Alpexpo, welcoming over 650 attendees.

The unconference

The unconference

A conference is more than just attending presentations, it’s also the discussions with the other participants, meeting the speakers… What better to continue these exchanges other than a day in the open air? SnowCamp ends with a day on the skiing slopes, in order to take advantage of the exceptional surroundings of Grenoble.
